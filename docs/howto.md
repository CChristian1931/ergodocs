# Tutorials

## What is a How To Guide?
> problem-oriented. 

Guides that take the user through a series of steps to achieve a common problem. 

## What matters?
- A series of steps
- A focus on the goal
- Addressing a specific question
- A little flexibility
- Practical usability
- Good naming
